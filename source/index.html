About Us

Who is Junction Networks?

Founded in 2004, Junction Networks delivers business VoIP communications services based on the best of breed IP telephony technologies. The company was founded on the premise that the future of business communications would rely heavily on IP technologies. Since 2004, Junction Networks has provided high quality business VoIP service to thousands of small, medium and large companies.

The company is managed by Internet veterans who previously built one of the largest Internet Service Providers in New York City, Interport Communications, before being purchased by RCN in the late 1990's.
